ABOUT US

Our proposed product named “Tokens.ph” is a cryptocurrency
exchange which aims to provide service for people to learn more about crypto
currency and to have a digital exchange platform where they can do more
transactions but spend less. Our vision is to create a safe space for traders
and crypto beginners where they can be assured that their money will be
secured, and they can easily transact to people around the world without
worrying about the interest rates.

OUR TEAM

Tokens.ph is proposed by our dear founders Cherise Pineda and
Nathaniel Bomaras in 2022, including a team of developers, computer
engineers, finance managers and data scientists who share the same passion
for helping people bring their analytical, critical, and investor minds to rise.
Tokens.ph have created and will create millions of transactions around the
globe and will continue to innovate and give a quality service to its users.
